Lionel Messi



Messi started playing football as a boy and in
1995 joined the youth team of Newell's Old B
oys (a Rosaro-based top-division football club).
Messi's phenomenal skills garnered the attention
of p restigious clubs on both sides of the Atlantic.
At age 13 Messi and his family relocated to
Barcelona, and he began playing for FC
Barcelona's under-14 team. He scored 21 goals
in 14 games for the junior team, and he quickly
graduated through the higher-level teams until
at age 16 he was given his informal debut with
FC Barcelona in a friendly match
